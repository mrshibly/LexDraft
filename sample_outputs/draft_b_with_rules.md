## 1. MATTER OVERVIEW

This matter involves a notice of material breach sent by Hartman & Associates LLP to Redline Ventures Inc. regarding a Service Agreement dated January 15, 2024 [1]. The notice was issued on May 3, 2024, under matter reference HA-2024-0089 [1]. Hartman & Associates is providing formal notice of Redline's failure to remit payment for professional services [1,2].

## 4. CORE DISPUTE / SUBJECT MATTER

Redline Ventures Inc. has failed to remit payment for professional services rendered during the period of February 1, 2024 through April 30, 2024, in violation of Section 4.2 of the Service Agreement [2]. The total outstanding amount is $47,500.00, with accrued interest of $712.50 as of the date of the notice [2,3].
